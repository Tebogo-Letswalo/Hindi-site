List of Digital Learning Conferences Going Virtual Due to Coronavirus

Digital learning conferences worldwide are going virtual due to the pandemic. Here’s a list.

Heba Ledwon Apr 20th, 2020
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 9

As the pandemic and the global travel restrictions continue, many conferences and events on digital learning that we published at the beginning of this year have been cancelled or shifted to a virtual format.

Below is a list of upcoming virtual events. We’ll be updating the list as new ones appear and add the links to the recordings of the past events if available. If you know about more online conferences, please let us know in the comments.

Upcoming events:

The 11th International Learning analytics and Knowledge Conference
April 12 – 16, 2021

This is the yearly conference by the Society for Learning Analytics Research (SoLAR), offered to learning analytics researchers and practitioners to share learning analytics research and practice. This year’s edition is organised by University of California, Irvine with the thematic focus: “The impact we make: The contribution of learning analytics to learning”. The deadline for full and short research papers, practitioner reports, and workshop/tutorial proposal submissions is November 1st, 2020. Check more deadlines here. To check previous conference proceedings, please check this list.
