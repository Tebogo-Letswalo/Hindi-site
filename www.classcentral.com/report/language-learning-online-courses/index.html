180+ Free University Courses to Learn a New Language

Learn Spanish, Italian, English, Chinese, Korean, and other languages from these free online courses.

Pat Bowden Aug 10th, 2022
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 2

There are many language learning apps out there, but MOOCs can do one thing that these apps aren’t doing or can’t do — Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) can teach you about the culture of the parent country.

To get a sense of what I’m talking about, take a look at the intro video for edX’s Mandarin Chinese for Business course (currently archived, but you can still access course materials).

You can learn Chinese, Spanish, Japanese, Italian, French, and other languages from these free university online courses. Class Central has created a collection of 200+ such MOOCs that teach more than 15 different languages.

And if you want to learn English, you can find plenty of resources and courses in Learn English as a Second Language (ESL) with Hundreds of Courses and Sites.

We particularly recommend six highly rated courses that have made it onto Class Central’s The Best Online Courses of All Time list:

  • Introduction to Dutch (FutureLearn)
  • First Step Korean (Coursera)
  • Basic Spanish 1: Getting Started (edX)
  • Chinese for Beginners (Coursera)
  • Introduction to Frisian (FutureLearn)
  • Tsinghua Chinese: Start Talking with 1.3 Billion People (edX) – Currently archived, but you can still access course materials.

While many courses are constantly available, some are session-based. If your chosen course is not available right now, you can Follow the subject, institution, or provider to receive emails announcing upcoming courses.
